Aarav spent six years on the sell side covering financials and consumer staples before deciding that most investing advice was needlessly opaque. He now writes about markets the way he wishes someone had explained them to him at 22 — with the numbers intact and the jargon stripped out.

When he isn't reading annual reports, he's usually arguing about cricket statistics, which he insists are just another form of fundamental analysis.
